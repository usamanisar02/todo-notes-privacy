Privacy Policy for Todo & Notes

Last updated: March 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Todo & Notes is developed by Muhammad Usama (TN Tech Studio). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

2. Data Collection

Todo & Notes does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All your todos and notes are stored locally on your device only.

3. Local Storage

All data including todos, notes, reminders, and settings are stored locally on your device using Hive database. We have no access to this data.

4. Notifications

The App uses local notifications to remind you about your todos. These notifications are generated on your device and no data is sent to any server.

5. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — To send todo reminders
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — To schedule reminders at exact times
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — To restore reminders after device restart
  • VIBRATE — For notification vibration

6. Third Party Services

The App does not use any third party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect any data from users of any age.
